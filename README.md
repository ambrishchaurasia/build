# BUILD

**DID YOU KNOW:** Jotting down your tasks at the start of the day guarantees a much higher chance of actually completing them. 

**BUILD** is the app I personally needed. I took my daily pen-and-paper routine and turned it into a gamified habit tracker specifically built for **developers and students**. It combines the satisfaction of checking off daily tasks with the premium UI and aesthetics I wanted to look at every single day.

## What it does

- **Automated Developer Sync:** Connects directly with GitHub and LeetCode. It automatically tracks your commits and solved problems without any manual entry.
- **Quest System:** Spawn and conquer daily tasks across 4 core arenas: Coding, Projects, Fitness, and Habits.
- **Momentum & Gamification:** Earn XP, level up your developer persona, and maintain daily flame streaks to keep your momentum going.
